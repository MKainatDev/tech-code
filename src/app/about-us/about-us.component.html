ABOUT-US

STM is one of the largest distributors of books in the GCC

STM is one of the largest distributors of books in the GCC. Having supplied the region since 2006 we are one of the leading and most respected book distributors with clients spanning the entire GCC.

Our presence In the GCC countries(UAE, Oman, Bahrain, Qatar, Kuwait and Saudi Arabia),allows us to distribute both University and School level books to our various clients. In conjunction with our network of publishers; this allows us to contribute to a world class education to the region.

In addition to academic books, we also distribute general/tradebooks to various public and private libraries and retail booksellers/bookshops to encourage the culture of reading for both the young and old generation. We offer both fiction and non-fiction books for all ages.

Our strategically located logistics service points in the US and UK are positioned to service the GCC and African market in an efficient manner.

We represent more than 1000 publishers in the region and have the ability to supply books in all subject areas for Schools, Universities, Hospitals and Research Institutions, public/private libraries and retail operations.
